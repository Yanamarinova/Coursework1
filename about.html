About Naked Bakery

In our bakery we combine the joy of baking and art to create delicious and beautiful vegan and gluten free treats. We believe that no matter your alergies or diet choices you should be able to treat yourself. Be naughty when it comes to the treats but kind when it comes to the sourcing of ingredients.

All of out products are 100% vegan and most of out treats can also be made gluten-free.

Why Did We Become Vegan

Our decision to become vegan does not only eliminate the problem of allergens in out goods but it is also cruelty-free, better for the environment and yourself.
